Become a photograher

You need to read, study great photographs and experiment with various techniques that professional photographers talk about. One of the keys to better composition is to avoid putting the primary focus of your picture in the center.On most digital and iPhone cameras, you will find 2 parallel horizontal lines and 2 vertical parallel lines. They divide the picture into 9 segments. When you focus your camera on the primary image, don’t put it in the center– move it into one of the other segments. This simple technique will let you frame more intriguing captures.
